Hummus

Description

This recipe is delicious and extremely simple to make! You won't be able to buy hummus from the store anymore after you try it!

I love to eat it with cucumbers and homemade sourdough torillas.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans chickpeas
  • 2 tbsp tahini paste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1.5 tbsp lemon juice
  • 0.5 cup water
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Optional garnish: paprika

Steps

  1. Dump ingredients in blender
  2. Blend
